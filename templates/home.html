What Would Jean Grey Rap?

Fun With Markov Chains

What if X-Men character Jean Grey (aka Phoenix) started rapping in the style of your favorite song artist?


First, you'll find there is no one answer. Second, there's a reason why X-Men characters don't break out into song and dance like a Glee episode. Third, it doesn't hurt to explore the possibilities.


So look no further! You'll be happy to know that this web application was created to satiate your curiosity.


Type in the name of a song artist and hit the submit button to generate some rap lyrics that were likely cut from final production.
